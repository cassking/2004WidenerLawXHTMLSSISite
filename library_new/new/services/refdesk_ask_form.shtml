Have a Question? Ask A Librarian!

This service is available to members of the Widener University School of Law community (including alumni) only. We regret that we cannot answer questions from the public.

When submitting your question by email, please include as much information as possible, including your return email address. You must indicate if you are a student, staff member or alumnus. We will attempt to answer your question within 24 hours

Please complete the form below or call one of our Reference Librarians: 302-477-2114 in Wilmington, Delaware or 717-541-3934 in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. You can also e-mail us at: Law.LibRef@law.widener.edu
