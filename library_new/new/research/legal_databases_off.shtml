Off Campus Access to Legal Research Databases

In order to access the library databases from off campus you must be a current student, staff, or faculty member of Widener University with a valid Widener ID Card. You must also configure your browser to use the library's proxy server.

Some people have reported problems with Internet Explorer. If you cannot get the proxy server to work with Internet Explorer, please download the latest version of Netscape.

To avoid typos when entering the URL given in the instructions below, highlight and copy it, then paste as required.

Follow the instructions below to configure your browser:

Netscape Version 6.2 or higher (PC or Mac)

  1. Click on Edit.
  2. Select Preferences.
  3. Click the '+' to the left of Advanced.
  4. Click on Proxies.
  5. Click the Automatic proxy configuration URL button.
  6. Enter in the Automatic proxy configuration URL box: http://147.31.63.12:8080/proxy.pac
  7. Click Reload, then OK

Netscape Version 4.x (PC or Mac)

  1. Click on Edit.
  2. Select Preferences.
  3. Click the '+' to the left of Advanced.
  4. Click on Proxies.
  5. Click the Automatic proxy configuration button.
  6. Enter in the Configuration location (URL) box: http://147.31.63.12:8080/proxy.pac
  7. Click Reload, then OK

MS Internet Explorer Version 5 or 6 (PC only) - Dial-up access

  1. Click Tools.
  2. Select Internet Options.
  3. Click the Connections tab.
  4. If you have multiple connections listed under Dial-up settings, select the one you want to configure.
  5. On the right of the Dial-up settings box, click Settings.
  6. Check the box next to Use automatic configuration script.
  7. Enter in the Address box: http://147.31.63.12:8080/proxy.pac
  8. Click OK.
  9. Click OK again.

MS Internet Explorer Version 5 or 6 (PC only) - Cable/DSL

  1. Click Tools.
  2. Select Internet Options.
  3. Click the Connections tab.
  4. Click the LAN Settings button.
  5. Check the box next to Use automatic configuration script.
  6. Enter in the Address box: http://147.31.63.12:8080/proxy.pac
  7. Click OK.
  8. Click OK again.

After you have made the appropriate changes, reboot your computer. Once you have rebooted your computer, go back to the library's website and choose the database you want to search. You should be prompted to enter your name and your library barcode number from the back of your Widener ID card.

If you are asked for a password and not a name and barcode number, the configuration was not completed properly. Please make sure that you have followed the instructions correctly.

A Special Note about AOL, Compuserve, and MSN: If you have AOL, CompuServe, or MSN (Microsoft Network) as your internet service provider, you may be able to access the databases from off campus if you download the FULL VERSION of Netscape 7.1 and configure according to the instructions above. However, access is not guaranteed. If you need help accessing databases from off campus please call the Reference Desk: Delaware campus (302) 477-2114.
