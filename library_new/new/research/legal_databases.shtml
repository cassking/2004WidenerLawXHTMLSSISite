Legal Research Databases

Local access only: you must be a current student, staff, or faculty member of Widener University. How to Access Databases Off Campus

  • BNA databases Online versions of BNA publications.
  • Britannica Online
  • CALI This link is for home use only. CALI is already installed in the library and computer lab. Click Start, Programs, CALI to run the lessons.
  • CCH Internet Research NetWork Human resources and health law.
  • CCH Internet Research NetWork Banking, securities and business law.
  • CCH Internet Tax Research NetWork
  • Current Index to Legal Periodicals (CILP) Weekly index of recently published law review articles.
  • EbscoHost Scholarly, business, health and other journals. Also includes newspapers and general magazines.
  • Government Information on the Internet
  • Grolier Online Grolier Multimedia Encyclopedia & Encyclopedia Americana.
  • HeinOnline Selected full-text legal journals in PDF format. Usually covers each journal back to the first volume. Also includes scanned images of the Federal Register from 1962-1980.
  • IndexMaster Click on "Search" to begin. Search the index and table of contents of thousands of legal titles.
  • Index to Foreign Legal Periodicals
  • Index to Legal Periodicals and Books
  • Law Books in Print
  • Law Library Microform Consortium Older federal executive, judicial and legislative documents available online. Click "Choose" under Search to begin searching.
  • Leadership Directories The Yellow Books, corporate, government & legal directories, Click, "Log In Here" for access.
  • LegalTrac Index to legal journals and newspapers, includes selected full-text articles. LegalTrac replaces Legal Resource Index.
  • LexisNexis
  • LexisNexis Congressional
  • Loislaw See Reference Librarian for access code.
  • Mergent Online (formerly FIS Online) Business and financial information, Click "Enter Mergent Online" for access.
  • palawlibrary.com See Reference Librarian for password.
  • ProQuest General magazines and periodicals online.
  • RIA Checkpoint The first time you use RIA you must go to www.riahome.com/school from any computer on the Widener network and register for a password. Once you have a password you can use Checkpoint from anywhere by using this URL riacheckpoint.com.
  • STAT-USA Business, trade and economic information from the Dept. of Commerce.
  • United Nations Treaty Collection
  • Westlaw.com
