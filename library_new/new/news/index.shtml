Upcoming Events and Other News

  • April 18-24, 2004: Book Drive at all University Libraries
    Widener University's Office for Community Engagement, Wolfgram Memorial Library, and the Legal Information Center (both Wilmington and Harrisburg branches) are sponsoring a BOOK DRIVE to benefit elementary school children in Chester, Wilmington and Harrisburg public schools.

    In addition, the Harrisburg campus will be collecting novels and nonfiction books for adult readers who are residents in a domestic abuse shelter.

    We are asking for the donations of new and gently used books to be dropped off in any one of the University's libraries during National Library Week (April 18th-24th).

    For more information, please contact Monica Belfatti in the Office for Community Engagement at (610) 499-4566.

    Books can be dropped off any time through April 24th.
