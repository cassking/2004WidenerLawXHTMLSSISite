New Books in the Library

New Books in the Library is a monthly listing of new law books received at the Widener University School of Law Library. Books are arranged by month acquired and by topic. The section of the site is updated on or about the first of each month. Please send comments or suggestions to WebSupport. For more information, see the complete list of Recent Acquisitions. Also, take a look at books featured in Previous Months.
