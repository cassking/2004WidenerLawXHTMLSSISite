About the Law Library / Legal Information Center

The Legal Information Center (LIC) is the major law library in the state of Delaware and is recognized as one of the major law libraries in the region. The LIC's staff is committed to offering personalized attention, delivering outstanding service, and providing ongoing education through legal and related library training programs.
