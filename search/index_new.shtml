Getting Started with Widener Law Search

Can't find something on the Widener University School of Law Web site?

Type a few words describing what you're looking for into the search box. NWidener University School of Law's Google Search will then go out and find pages that match what you're looking for on all Widener Law affiliated pages. For example, if you were looking for Web pages about Moot Court, just type the term into a search box.

About Widener Law's Search Function

Widener's Search function is offered to insure that you get the best possible return for your keyword search. The Law School website has hundreds of pages catalogued by Google, a powerful Web metasearch engine that queries most of the Web's top search engines.

Searching Tips

Type in the keyword you are looking for. Click the 'Go Search' button.

Finding Faculty and Staff at Widener

Type in the last name of the person you are searching for and click the 'Go Search' button. Alternatively, visit our main Faculty page or our Law School Departments main page.
