Legal Education Institute Alumni News

We are pleased to announce the recent commencement of fifty-four Legal Education Institute students. In addition, eight students received their Associate's degree in Paralegal Studies, and sixteen students received their Bachelor's degree at a commencement held at the Chester campus on May 15, 2004.

Our certificate ceremony was held at the Barrister's club on Monday, May 17 at 6 o'clock. There were approximately 130 graduates, family members, and friends in attendance. Our guest speaker was Nadine M. Overton, Esq. who was joined by Douglas E. Ray, Dean & Vice President of the Widener University Law Center.

After the speakers' remarks, Professor Eileen Grena presented the awards for outstanding academic achievement to Linda Johnson (LNC certificate) and Derrick Lowe (Paralegal certificate). At an earlier ceremony, Rachel McGhee, who graduated in August 2003, was awarded the 2003-2004 University College Award for Outstanding Academic Achievement in Paralegal Studies. Ms. McGhee is also the honored recipient of the Presidential Scholarship to Widener School of Law and just recently completed her first year of law school.

At the conclusion of the certificate ceremony, Dean McCormick gave her closing remarks and challenged the recent graduates to celebrate their accomplishment, focus their sights on a new goal, and begin charting their course to success.
