Legal Education Institute Student Services
Services for Current Students

The Legal Education Institute offers students many opportunities to enhance their educational experience and to begin preparing for the transition into the legal working environment.

Some of the services include:

  • Writing Lab
  • Tutoring
  • Career Counseling
  • Student Organizations

Writing Lab

The writing lab is available to all students desiring to improve their written communication skills. Students meet one-on-one with an instructor who can evaluate the student's writing and make suggestions. Times are conveniently scheduled to accommodate both day and evening students.

Tutoring

Students needing additional assistance in any area of the program may participate in group or one-on-one tutoring provided by LEI instructors. Tutoring is available during day and evening hours and is provided at no extra cost to the student.

Career Counseling

All areas of career counseling are provided through a joint effort of LEI faculty and the Career Development Office (CDO). Group workshops and one-on-one assistance in the areas of resume writing, cover letter writing, interviewing, and skills assessments are available. In addition, regional listings of open positions in the legal field are maintained for student and alumni use.

Student Organizations

Students at LEI are eligible to participate in all Widener University student organizations.

The two most popular organizations among LEI students are the Student Paralegal Association (SPA) which meets at the Delaware campus and the Pre-Law Society which meets at the Chester campus.
