Welcome to the Legal Education Institute!

The Legal Education Institute (LEI) was established in 1990 as a cooperative effort between the Widener University School of Law and University College, Widener's evening and weekend division.

The Institute offers paraprofessional programs leading to the following degrees or certifications:



Paralegal Program:

  • Paralegal Studies Main Page
  • Bachelor of Science in Paralegal Studies
  • Associate of Science in Paralegal Studies
  • Certificate of Completion in Paralegal Studies

Legal Nurse Consultant (LNC) Program:

  • Legal Nurse Consultant Main Page
  • Certificate of Completion in Legal Nurse Consulting

Internships:

  • Internships Main Page

All these programs are offered in an environment that is fully integrated with the School of Law. The Paralegal Studies and the Legal Nurse Consultant programs are approved by the American Bar Association.

Academic Reputation

Widener University has earned a reputation for excellent academic programs taught in an environment that stresses personal attention for each of its students. Our classes are small so that each student is known by his or her name, not as a number. Our distinguished faculty and experienced staff are devoted to providing each student with the skills and opportunities necessary to develop individual talents. Our educational resources are among the best in the nation.

Whether you are considering our Paralegal Studies or Legal Nurse Consultant program, you will enter a challenging and rewarding profession. You also will find that we offer flexibility in class scheduling to meet your needs and objectives at the Legal Education Institute of Widener University.

The student body is composed of individuals from a variety of cultural and educational backgrounds. Students are enriched by this diversity through the life and work experience of their colleagues.
