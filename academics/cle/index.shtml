Continuing Legal Education at Widener Law

The School of Law develops and conducts programs of continuing legal education (CLE) to reinforce the skills and update the knowledge of practicing attorneys. In addition to the Widener faculty, prominent members of the bench and bar teach the CLE courses.

Widener has been certified by the Pennsylvania Supreme Court AND THE DELAWARE COMMISSION ON CONTINUING LEGAL EDUCATION as an official provider for continuing legal education in Pennsylvania. In this capacity, the School conducts numerous CLE programs in Pennsylvania and Delaware to serve those attorneys who require state-mandated CLE.
