Office of the Registrar

Welcome! Welcome to the Registrar's Office at Widener University School of Law. This office oversees student records. This includes course registration, bar applications, examinations and applications for graduation and degree conferrals. The Law School's Registrar's Office is the only office that can supply official transcripts. In this section you will find information about: course registration, schedules, first assignments, emergency announcements (when active), transcript requests and much more.
