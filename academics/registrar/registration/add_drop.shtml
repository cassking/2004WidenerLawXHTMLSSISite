Add/Drop Information & Important Dates for 2004-2005

Add/Drop Procedure

For the Law Center, the students complete registration utilizing an on-line system. Each matriculated student receives a Campus Cruiser login and password, which allows access to the on-line SERVICES tab. Information regarding HOW TO REGISTER ON-LINE is available on the website. Add/Drop is completed by the student in person at the Registrar's Office during the first week classes for the fall and spring semesters. Registration on-line is permitted for the summer session until the second day of the summer term.

Please refer to the Student Handbook for further clarification regarding registration and withdrawal from courses.
