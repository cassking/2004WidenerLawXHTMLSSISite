Registrar Forms & Downloads

The Registrar's Office maintains official student records for Widener Law students. If you need a transcript or certification of enrollment, need to change your name or address, or report a change in your employment status, please complete the appropriate forms found on this page. Our forms are available in either MSExcel, MSWord or PDF. (What is a PDF?)
