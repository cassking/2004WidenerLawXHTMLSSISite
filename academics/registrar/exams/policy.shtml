Our Policy for the Delaware and Harrisburg Campuses

The ExamSoft Signup period is open during designated times ONLY.

Note: Students who are returning users of ExamSoft with active accounts can redownload SofTest by logging in to the Widener Law ExamSoft® page after clicking 'Exam Takers' at the bottom.

If you would like to use your laptop to take your final examinations, you must provide written notice to the Registrar’s Office no later than the designated deadline for each semester. You may obtain the registration form from the Registrar’s Office or use our Online Request Form. You will be required to list your name, social security number, the exams you will be taking with your laptop, your telephone number and e-mail address.

The software must be downloaded no later than the designated deadline for each semester. Once you have downloaded, you must complete the required qualification exam.

The cost is $20.00 for the academic year, which includes all exams taken during the Fall, Spring and Summer. The cost is the same no matter how many exams you take. ExamSoft will supply the floppy disk, print the completed examination and deliver it to your professors.
