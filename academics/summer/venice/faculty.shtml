Venice, Italy Summer Abroad Law Institute: Faculty

Barry R. Furrow

Barry R. Furrow, B.A. Harvard College, J.D. Harvard Law School. Director, Venice Summer International Law Institute. Professor of Law and Director, the Health Law Institute, Widener University School of Law; Professor (adjunct), University of the Sciences in Philadelphia. Co-author of a leading casebook on American health law; a two-volume practitioners treatise on American health law; and more than three dozen articles on health law topics.

Fabrizio Marrella

Fabrizio Marrella, S.J.D., summa cum laude, University of Bologna and the University of Paris I (Panthéon Sorbonne); Diploma, The Hague Academy of International Law. Co-Director, Venice Summer International Law Institute; Professor of International and Admiralty Law at the Department of Law of the University of Venice; and Adjunct Professor of International Trade Law at the Institut des Hautes Etudes de l'Amérique Latine of the University of Paris III - La Sorbonne Nouvelle.

Professor Juliet Moringiello

Professor Juliet Moringiello, B.S.F.S. Georgetown University School of Foreign Service, J.D. Fordham University and LL.M. Temple University. Professor Moringiello teaches courses in bankruptcy, Uniform Commercial Code, and electronic commerce. She is the Co-chair of the Electronic Commerce Subcommittee of the Cyberspace Consumer Law of the Association of American Law Schools; an elected member of the American Law Institute. She has also taught in Widener’s Summer International Law Institutes in Geneva, Switzerland and Sydney Australia.
