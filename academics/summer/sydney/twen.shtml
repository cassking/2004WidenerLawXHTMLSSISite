Sydney, Australia Summer Abroad Law Institute: The West Education Network (TWEN)

Students accepted to this year's Sydney program are encouraged to visit the Sydney Program's TWEN site for the most up-to-date information about the program.

Students should visit http://www.lawschool.westlaw.com, click on 'TWEN', and add the Widener Law School Sydney Program to their Westlaw homepage.

The West Education Network (TWEN) is an electronic extension of the classroom, integrating academic tools, Westlaw research, and other resources in an online environment.
