Sydney, Australia Summer Abroad Law Institute: Housing

Students are responsible for making their own residential arrangements. The UTS Accommodation Service ( http://www.housing.uts.edu.au ) provides students the assistance they need. As well, Widener University School of Law's International Programs Coordinator can assist you with establishing contact with the UTS Housing Service, should you have difficulty. Contact Margaret Wyant at (302) 477-2248 should you require assistance. The city of Sydney also offers an online apartment service at the following link: http://www.apartmentservice.com.au/sydney.htm The website offers several attractive options for students with particular housing needs, and has been used with great success by students who have attended the program in recent years.

The facilities listed below are recommended facilities in the city of Sydney. Students are not required to use any of these facilities, and can make individual arrangements should another facility be more attractive to them. After comprehensive consultation with UTS, Sydney, this list represents trusted accommodations in the immediate area surrounding the university. Please keep in mind that the dollar figures listed are estimates, and subject to conversion/exchange to U.S. dollars. A general rule of thumb is to estimate Australian dollar figures as 3/4 of U.S. dollars. For example, $200 per night Australian converts to approximately $150 U.S. Please consult the websites listed for more information about the respective facility.
