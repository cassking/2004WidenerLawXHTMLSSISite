Geneva, Switzerland Summer Abroad Law Institute: Attractions

"The Geneva program offered the perfect schedule for the summer - four days of challenging and stimulating course work, followed by a three-day weekend to allow for travel around Europe."

» Ann M. McGee '98 Widener University School of Law

Switzerland is bordered by France, Germany, Austria, Italy and tiny Liechtenstein. Geneva's central location places most of the great cities and regions of Europe within reach; Paris, Milan, Florence, Venice, Brussels, Munich, and Innsbruck are easy weekend journeys. Adventuresome students can reach Vienna, Berlin, Amsterdam, Prague, or Barcelona in a day (or night) by train.

Switzerland itself has four official languages and corresponding cultures: French, German, Italian, and Romansch. Each of these regions is easily accessible in a few hours travel from Geneva. Students in the Geneva Institute will have ample opportunity to travel and explore Switzerland and the rest of Europe both on the weekends and before and after class.
