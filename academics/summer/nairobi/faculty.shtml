Nairobi, Kenya Summer Abroad Law Institute: Faculty

James Gathii

James Gathii, LL.B., University of Nairobi; LL.M., and S.J.D., Harvard University Law School. Assistant Professor of Law, Albany Law School of Union University.

Patricia Kameri-Mbote

Patricia Kameri-Mbote, LL.B., University of Nairobi; LL.M. University of Warwick; S.J.D. Stanford University. Senior Lecturer, Faculty of Law, University of Nairobi. Advocate of High Court of Kenya. Fellow, African Centre for Technology Studies.

J. Patrick Kelly

J. Patrick Kelly, J.D., Harvard University Law School; B.A. University of Delaware. Professor of International Law, Widener University School of Law; Fulbright Professor of Law, Makerere University , Kampala, Uganda (1980-82); Director and Founder, Nairobi International Law Institute.

Kivutha Kibwana

Kivutha Kibwana, LL.B., University of Nairobi; LL.M., University of London; LL.M. Harvard University Law School; S.J.D., George Washington University National Law Center. Associate Professor of Law, Faculty of Law, University of Nairobi. He was recently elected to Parliament in December, 2002 and serves as Assistant Minister in the Office of the President.

Githu Muigai

Githu Muigai, LL.B., University of Nairobi; LL.M., Columbia University School of Law. Senior Lecturer in Law, Faculty of Law, University of Nairobi. Advocate of the High Court of Kenya and member of the Constitutional Review Conference.

Albert Mumma

Albert Mumma, LL.B., University of Nairobi; LL.M., Yale University; Ph.D. in Environmental Law, Cambridge University. Lecturer in Law, Faculty of Law, University of Nairobi. He has published extensively in the areas of international environmental law and water pollution including a new text from McGraw-Hill.
