For PC Users: Use the following steps, to restore Windows Media Player as your default media program:

  1. Click on the "Start" button in the lower left corner of your computer's screen.
  2. Slide the arrow up the "Start" menu and click on the "Programs" button.
  3. Locate "Windows Media Player" in the programs list, and click on it
  4. After Windows Media Player Opens, Click on "Tools" in the menu bar.
  5. Slide the arrow down until you reach "Options" and click on it.
  6. A small window should open up on your screen, comprised of several tabs (including): a. Player b. Network c. CD Audio Portable Device d. Formats e. Etc...
  7. Click on the tab entitled "Formats"
  8. Once in this tab you should see a list of file associations. Just below the file associations and to the right you should see a "Select All" button.
  9. Click the "Select All" button.
  10. Click on the "Apply Button."
  11. Click the "OK" button, which will be located to the left of the "Apply Button"
  12. The window that contained all the tabs will automatically close.
  13. Finally, return to our website and click on the "Play Movie" link of your choice.
