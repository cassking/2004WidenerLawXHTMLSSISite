Nairobi, Kenya Summer Abroad Law Institute: Mount Kilimanjaro

Each year several of the students make the five day trek up and down the roof of Africa. At 19,000 feet Kili is the highest mountain in Africa and a beautiful hike through several ecological zones. Students typically arrange the trip during the week off which this year extends from July 6-13. There will be further details in the Manual and a discussion of options when you arrive.
