Nairobi, Kenya Summer Abroad Law Institute: Housing

We will send each accepted student a copy of our comprehensive manual (and offer it as a PDF file available for download in Spring 2004) which describes in detail how to prepare for the trip and what to expect during your stay. For next year we have arranged excellent housing at an apartment building behind the Sarit Center in Westlands about one mile from the Faculty of Law. Bid Apartments is a series of low rise apartment with balconies in a fully protected gated compound. The security is excellent.

Members of the Institute will be able to share one and two bedroom apartments or rent a studio for one person. The accommodations include a full kitchen, living room, TV, telephone, and a swimming pool. It is only a few steps from the Sarit Center, a large modern shopping center with a large supermarket, restaurants, and movie theaters. A shared room will cost approximately $500-$700 for the seven weeks of the program. Single rooms are available for a supplement.

If you wish to be closer to the Faculty of Law, we have arranged for rooms at the Hotel Sirona, a small comfortable hotel only one and half blocks from your classes. The hotel has a restaurant and bar.
