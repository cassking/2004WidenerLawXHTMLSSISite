Harrisburg Criminal Defense Clinic

Harrisburg, Pennsylvania

For an indigent person charged with a crime, the Widener student attorney standing alongside is as important a person as there is. The Pennsylvania Criminal Defense Clinic is a law office that allows Widener students to put into practice all of the criminal law and procedure that they have absorbed in their first and second year courses to bear on behalf of clients in need of their services.

Professor Judy Ritter teaches her students the finer points of criminal defense work, along with the lawyering skills needed to represent Clinic clients from preliminary hearing through bench or jury trial -and beyond. In addition to their regular practice before the Pennsylvania state courts of Chester County (West Chester), Widener student attorneys regularly join Professor Ritter in post-conviction representation of defendants on death row, crafting briefs to be filed with the Supreme Court of the United States.
