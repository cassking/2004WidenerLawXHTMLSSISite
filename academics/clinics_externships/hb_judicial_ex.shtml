Harrisburg Judicial Externship Program

Harrisburg, Pennsylvania

In the Judicial Externship Program, a student may obtain credit by working in the chambers of a judge, observing judicial proceedings, drafting documents and conducting legal research for the court. Placements of this nature are ideally suited for students who wish to enhance their writing skills and gain first-hand knowledge of the inner workings of a specific court system. As with the Clinical Externship Program, students attend a weekly classroom component and may obtain two, three or four credit hours for their work in the placement. Students are encouraged to choose placements in their own communities in order to familiarize themselves with their local legal community if they wish to practice there upon graduation. Many of these placements result in contacts and references for future employment.

Widener Harrisburg Students have been placed with:

  • Supreme Court of Pennsylvania: http://www.courts.state.pa.us/Index/Supreme/indexSupreme.asp
  • Superior Court of Pennsylvania: http://www.courts.state.pa.us/Index/Superior/IndexSuperior.asp
  • Commonwealth Court: http://www.courts.state.pa.us/Index/Cwealth/indexCwealth.asp
  • U.S. District Court: http://www.pamd.uscourts.gov/
  • Court of Common Pleas, Commonwealth of PA in Adams, Berks, Cumberland, Dauphin, Lackawanna, Lehigh, Luzerne, Northumberland, Wayne and York counties.
