Delaware Civil Clinic

Wilmington, Delaware

The Delaware Civil Clinic provides free legal services to indigent clients with civil legal problems. It operates in conjunction with Delaware Volunteer Legal Services (http://www.dvls.org/), the pro bono arm of the Delaware Bar Association (http://www.dsba.org/). Students are admitted to the limited practice of law under Delaware Supreme Court Rule #56 and represent clients in Justice of the Peace Court, Family Court and before administrative tribunals.

Clinic students learn to apply the practical lawyering skills of interviewing, counseling, negotiating and trial advocacy in the representation of actual clients. Clinic students handle family law cases including domestic violence matters, landlord tenant cases, wills, Power of Attorney, and living wills, as well as other legal issues.
