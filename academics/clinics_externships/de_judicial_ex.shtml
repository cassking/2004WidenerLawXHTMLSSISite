Delaware Judicial Externship Program

Wilmington, Delaware

The Delaware Valley is the home of some of the best respected courts in the United States. In addition to the fine state trial courts of Pennsylvania, Delaware and New Jersey, the metropolitan areas of Wilmington, Philadelphia, Camden and Trenton are home to justices of the Supreme Courts of all three states, along with such specialized state courts as the highly-respected Delaware Court of Chancery (http://courts.state.de.us/Courts/Court%20of%20Chancery/) and the Equity Division of the New Jersey Superior Court.

In the federal system, judges of the United States Court of Appeals fo r the third circuit sit in Philadelphia and Wilmington, as do judges in the United States District Court, Magistrate Court and Bankruptcy Court.

In all of these courts you will find students from the Judicial Externship Program. Widener students receive academic credit while spending a full y ear serving as law clerks, working closely with judges who have graciously agreed to serve as mentors. The judges help the externs with objectives for their placements and for their careers, provide challenging research assignments, help externs hone their legal writing, and offer a unique perspective on the work of the courts. These placements often result in contacts and references for future employment.
