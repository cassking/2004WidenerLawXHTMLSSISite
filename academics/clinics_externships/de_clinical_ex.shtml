Delaware Clinical Externship Program

Wilmington, Delaware

The Delaware Valley is a region rich with varied opportunities for students of the practice of law. Every year Widener sends as many as 30 students who are enrolled in the Clinical Externship Program to government and public service law offices, where they spend the full year receiving academic credit while learning and working alongside attorneys carefully chosen for their skill, dedication and qualities as mentors. These placements often result in contacts and references for future employment. They always result in a year of critical reflection as student attorneys meet regularly in small groups with Professor Larry Connell and Professor Fran Catania to discuss issues of legal doctrine and skills; of professional and personal ethics; and of the culture of a modern law office.
