Health Law: Elective Courses

Health law graduate students may choose from the upper-level electives in the law school catalog. Such electives will typically include the following, although not all are offered in a given year:

  • Advanced Corporations
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution
  • Antitrust
  • Business Acquisitions
  • Business Organizations
  • Business Tax Planning I
  • Business Tax Planning II
  • Children and the Law
  • Complex Litigation
  • Corporate Finance
  • Disability Law
  • Domestic Violence
  • Employment Discrimination Law
  • Employment Law
  • Family Law
  • Government Contracts
  • Labor Law
  • Law and Economics
  • Legislation
  • Products Liability
  • White Collar Crime
