Master of Jurisprudence (M.J.) in Health Law

The Master of Jurisprudence (M.J.) in Health Law is designed for health care and other professionals who desire to expand their education and to gain a broadened understanding of the legal and policy health care environment. This degree will equip professionals for regulatory and compliance work, risk management, contract negotiation, and other health law related employment, in hospitals, pharmaceutical companies and managed care organizations.
