Adjunct Faculty

  • The Honorable Jack Jacobs - Vice Chancellor, Delaware Court of Chancery, Adjunct Professor. Vice Chancellor Jack Jacobs received his Bachelor of Arts degree from University of Chicago and his Juris Doctor from Harvard University. He teaches a class on advanced corporation law. He has served as a Vice Chancellor in the court of Chancery since 1985. Prior to joing the Court of Chancery, he was a partner at Young, Conaway, Stargatt, and Taylor, a large Wilmington firm. Vice Chancellor Jacobs is a member of the American Law Institute.
  • The Honorable William Quillen - Judge, Delaware Superior Court Adjunct Professor. Judge William Quillen received a Bachelor of Arts degree from Williams College, a Juris Doctor from Harvard University, and a LL.M from the University of Virgina. During his long and illustrious career he has served on the Delaware Supreme Court and Court of Chancery, and he currently sits on the Delaware Superior Court. Judge Quillen was also a partner at a major Wilmington firm and for two years was Delaware's Secretary of State.
