Corporate Law & Finance

As the only law school located in Delaware, Widener offers its graduate students a corporate law education of unparalleled quality. Consider just a few of the advantages of Widener's LL.M. in Corporate law and Finance.

  • Full-time faculty members who litigated landmark cases
  • Adjunct faculty members from the Delaware judiciary
  • Opportunities to watch the Delaware courts in action
  • Lectures by leading corporate law scholars
  • An opportunity to participate in Widener's Delaware Journal of Corporate Law
