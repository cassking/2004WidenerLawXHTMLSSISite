Bar Programs at Widener Law: Focusing On Passing the Bar

Widener offers its students two programs to help you pass the bar:

  • Widener Delaware Bar Review: This course assists candidates in preparing for the Delaware Bar Examination, utilizing Bar/Bri multistate materials as well as special 3-volume copyright materials on Delaware state law prepared by our course instructors exclusively for our students.
  • Widener Bar Readiness Program(WBRP): The WBRP is a spring bar lecture and testing program, presented by law school faculty and Barbri of PA/NJ, covering the six multistate bar examination subjects and other bar related topics, and includes five practice test opportunities.
