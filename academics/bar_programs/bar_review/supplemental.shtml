Delaware Bar Review Program: Supplemental Courses

Professional Multistate Bar Review (PMBR)

A 3-day supplemental multistate workshop is available for an additional $240 to students enrolled in the 2004 Widener Delaware Bar Review Course.

The course is tentatively scheduled for June 21-23, 2004.

This discounted PMBR rate (cost is normally $395) is available only to those students enrolled in the Widener Delaware Bar Review and who register for PMBR through the Widener Delaware Bar Review.

Registration for PMBR will be accepted up to June 21, 2004.
