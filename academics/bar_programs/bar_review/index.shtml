Delaware Bar Review Program

Location & Dates

When: May 19, 2004 through July 13, 2004

Where: Widener University School of Law Wilmington, Delaware

What: 2004 Widener Delaware Bar Review Course

Objective:To assist candidates in preparing for the Delaware Bar Examination, utilizing Bar/Bri multistate materials as well as special 3-volume copyright materials on Delaware state law prepared by our course instructors exclusively for our students.

Contact Information: visit the Contact Us page
