Delaware Bar Readiness Program: Costs & Fees

While the cost of the program will be borne by the law school, participants who intend to submit any practice essay exam for grading and feedback will be required to pay a $16.00 examination fee. This fee is assessed by Barbri to compensate essay graders. The fee must be prepaid and is non-refundable, and permits the participant to submit four practice essays for grading and feedback. Barbri has approximated a two-week turn around time for each essay submitted for scoring.
