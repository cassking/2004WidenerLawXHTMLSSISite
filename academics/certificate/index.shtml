Certificate Programs at Widener Law: Focusing On Individual Areas of Law

In recognition of the professional advantages gained through concentrated study, the Widener faculty has adopted certificate programs in Health Law, Law and Government, Business Organizations Law and Trial Advocacy. Although these concentrations have been recently formalized, the courses, activities and opportunities they provide to Widener students have been part of Widener and its curriculum for many years; indeed, some have existed since the inception of the Law School.

  • Delaware Campus:
  • The Health Law Institute: Health Law Certificate Program
  • The Institute of Delaware Corporate Law: Business Organizations Law Certificate Program
  • The Trial Advocacy Institute: Certificate in Trial Advocacy Program
  • Harrisburg Campus:
  • The Law and Government Institute: Law & Government Certificate Programs
