Welcome to Widener University School of Law

Widener is a unique law school in that we have two campuses. Both provide an excellent legal education and our two campus arrangement adds strength and support to each. Both campuses are located in legal centers that offer many opportunities for full-time and part-time legal employment as well as internships with government agencies and courts.
