Checking Your Financial Aid Status Online

Financial Aid information is posted on Campus Cruiser every semester.

  • How to use Campus Cruiser logins and passwords to get your financial aid information
  • The financial aid information you can view includes:
  • General account summary
  • Account summary by term

Using Campus Cruiser to Check Your Financial Aid Status

  • Log-in to Campus Cruiser using your login and password
  • Click on the 'Services' tab at the top of your page.
  • Click Financial Profile on the left side of the screen
  • Choose to view either 'Account Summary' or 'Account Summary by Term'
