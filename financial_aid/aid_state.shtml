State Financial Aid Programs

The programs outlined below are subject to change based upon federal and state regulations.

Delaware Scholarship Incentive Program

Delaware Residents: Delaware Scholarships are awarded on the basis of need to state residents in varying amounts up to $1,000 per year. Academically, law students must carry a minimum of nine credits per semester and maintain a 2.5 cumulative grade point average. Applicants must have FAFSA results processed by April 15. No application is necessary. Results of FAFSA will automatically be released to the state if you indicate you are a permanent resident of Delaware.
