The Widener Scholars Program

The Widener Scholars Program provides a number of scholarships for entering students whose credentials rank them in the top of the Law School's applicants for admission. These awards range from renewable full tuition scholarships to non-renewable $2,000.00 awards. Scholarships are awarded by the Scholarship Committee at or soon after admission and are continued for the entire School of Law career of the recipient if the recipient achieves suitable law school grades.

To recognize outstanding law school achievement for the first year of study, the Widener Scholars Program awards several major scholarships. Awards are made in the summer after completion of the first academic year. The scholarships are applied to the students' second year tuition and fees and may be continued for subsequent years if the recipient maintains suitable academic performance.
