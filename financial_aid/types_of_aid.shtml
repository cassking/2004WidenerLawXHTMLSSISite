Types of Financial Aid Available For Your Education

The Office of Financial Aid works with any student wishing assistance in finding appropriate sources of funding. Funding sources vary, but may include, low interest government subsidized loans, non subsidized government loans, private loans, institutional and private scholarships and grants. The Financial Aid Office works to develop the best possible funding package for each student depending upon the students eligibility for various programs.

  • Widener Scholars Program. The Widener Scholars Program provides a number of scholarships for entering students whose credentials rank them in the top of the Law School's applicants for admission.
  • Federal Financial Aid. Work-Study, Perkins loans and Stafford loans.
  • State Financial Aid. Delaware scholarship program.
  • Private Financial Aid. A number of donor scholarships, private and alternative loan programs.
