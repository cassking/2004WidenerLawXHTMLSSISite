Job Search: Classified Ads Web Sites

Classified sites post available positions in specific regions of the country or areas of law. They are usually most useful to experienced attorneys, but we note sites which list student or recent graduate opportunities as well.

  • Association of Patent Law Firms http://www.aplf.org/ Job Bank with experienced attorney and students positions. Links to member patent law firms.
  • Attorney Jobs on Line http://www.attorneyjobs.com/ West Group web site with jobs for attorneys and law students. Requires Westlaw password. Database contains only attorney jobs and law-related jobs (JD preferred) for attorneys, in every practice area, with every type of employer, throughout the U.S. and abroad. In addition, it contains information on judicial clerkships, fellowships, legal search /headhunter positions, and government contract opportunities. Listings include full-time, part-time, temporary, contract, as well as other kinds of special situations. Click to, download, and print over 400 special application forms from the U.S. Government, state & local government, and international organizations. You will not have to wade through job listings for non-lawyers.
  • Deadlines For Federal Employment http://www.law.duke.edu/career/fedagency.html Lists deadlines for applying to federal agencies.
  • Emplawyernet http://www.emplawyernet.com/ Must join and become member to gain access to specific job listings, some for students. Useful job-search information. Links to firms with recruitment websites.
  • Environmental Organization Web Directory http://www.webdirectory.com/ Links to environmental job sites. Addresses for environmental employers.
  • Federal Law Clerk Information System https://lawclerks.ao.uscourts.gov Searchable database of federal law clerk openings. Relatively new site that has not yet been fully utilized by the federal courts.
  • Federal Jobs Digest http://www.jobsfed.com/ Database of federal vacancies, including legal; some available for students. Resume submission feature. Description of federal benefits.
  • Job Links for Lawyers http://home.sprynet.com/~ear2ground/ Contains multiple links to sites posting current openings for lawyers.
  • Juris Resources Database http://www.jurisresources.com/ Searchable headhunter database of law jobs for experienced attorneys.
  • Lawinfo Employment Center http://jobs.lawinfo.com/ Classified's for experienced attorneys, paralegals and other law-related positions. Accepts resumes from those seeking employment.
  • Lawmatch http://lawmatch.com/ Open access job and resume bank for legal jobs.
  • Legal Recruiter http://www.legalrecruiters.com/ Classified's for experienced attorneys. Receive free e-mail notifications of new job positngs.
  • JDPost http://www.jdpost.com/ Password protected; free to users who register. Classified's for law students and recent graduates. Databases of legal employers and judges. Housing sublets, rentals and roommates.
  • Nationjob http://www.nationjob.com/legal/ Searchable national database of legal jobs.
  • Office of Personnel Management http://www.usajobs.opm.gov/ Database of federal government jobs and internships, including experienced attorney and student positions.
  • PSLawnet http://www.pslawnet.org/ Password protected; free to Widener users who register. The best available database of public interest internships, fellowships and attorney jobs on the web. Listings for law students, recent grads and experienced attorneys. Positions are listed from private, public and government employers throughout the U.S. and world.
  • U.S. Department of Justice http://www.usdoj.gov/ Attorney positions. Honors Program for students. Summer Internships for students
  • Yahoo Listing of Legal Headhunter Web Sites http://dir.yahoo.com Large listing of web sites for legal recruiting firms.
