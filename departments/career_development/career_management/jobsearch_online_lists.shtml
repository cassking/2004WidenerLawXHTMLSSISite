Job Search: Listservs

Listservs are devoted to a specific subject or group of people. Most lists are open for anyone to join. When you subscribe to a listserv you receive a copy of every message that is posted to the list. This can result in several messages a day.

  • Fedjobs This is a Federal Job Bulletin Board and has postings from the U.S. Office of Personnel Management. It is updated weekly and has approximately 7,500 subscribers. To subscribe: send the following email message to listserv@listserv.dartmouth.edu subscribe fedjobs your name
  • Lawsch-L This forum is for discussions relating to law school. Most of the members are law students. To subscribe: send the following email message to listserv@american.edu subscribe lawsch-L your name
  • Lawjobs-L This is a discussion group for job announcements in any law-related profession. To subscribe: send the following email message to, listserv@lawlib.wuacc.edu subscribe lawjobs-L your name
