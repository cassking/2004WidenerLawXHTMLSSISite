Public Service Opportunities: An Overview of Clinics & Externships

Widener School of Law provides a variety of resources for students interested in pursuing careers in public interest. The Law School has a long tradition of public service and community involvement, and supports students who are interested in pursuing public interest careers.

Clinics
Widener School of Law is home to five clinics: the Delaware Civil Clinic, Environmental Natural Resource Clinic, Pennsylvania Civil Clinic, Pennsylvania Criminal Defense Clinic, and the Harrisburg Civil Law Clinic. These clinics give students the opportunity to gain "hands on" experience, while providing an important public service.

Clinical and Judicial Externships
Our Clinical Externship Programs in Delaware and Harrisburg send as many as 30 students a year to government and public service law offices.
