Career Management Resources

Career planning enables students to enter fields as diverse as their interests. Widener graduates have pursued a multitude of career paths encompassing a wide spectrum of traditional legal occupations, including United States attorneys, Judge Advocate General (JAG), judges, corporate attorneys and partners in private law firms. Widener graduates have also gone on to secure many nontraditional legal positions, serving as members of Congress, presidents of large corporations, broadcast journalists and law school professors.
