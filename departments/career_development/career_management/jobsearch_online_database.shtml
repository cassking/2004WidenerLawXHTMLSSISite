Job Search: Attorney Databases

When you need to look up an individual lawyer or search for lawyers of a certain type, turn to these databases.

  • Martindale Hubbell http://lawyers.martindale.com/Executable/Location.php3/ One of the most comprehensive listings of attorneys worldwide. Search database for individuals, firms, government and corporate attorneys. Excellent resource for finding firms' web sites, as they are listed and linked from firm pages.
  • West Legal Directory http://directory.findlaw.com/ Search database for information on firms, government offices, corporate law offices and lawyers in the U. S. and Canada. Articles on legal developments. Descriptions of practice areas and state-specific searches for lawyers by practice area.
