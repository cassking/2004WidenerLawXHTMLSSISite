Post A Job In Our Job Bank

We maintain an on-line and printed Job Bank that is used by current students and Widener Law graduates. All job openings we receive throughout the year are quickly entered into our Job Bank. The Job Bank can be used for permanent, full-time positions (entry-level or lateral) and for summer clerkships, part-time positions, and one-time-only research projects.

You may post a law-related job through either of our campuses.

  • Contact the Career Development staff at our Wilmington, Delaware campus
  • Contact the Career Development staff at our Harrisburg, Pennsylvania campus

We look forward to working with you in your recruiting efforts.
