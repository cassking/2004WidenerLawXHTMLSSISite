Career Development Office Recruitment Guide for Employers

Placement Services for Employers

The Career Development Office offers a variety of options to assist you in your search for law students and attorneys.

Fall and Spring On-Campus Recruitment Programs
Widener is unique in that it offers employers the opportunity to interview students from both our campuses at one location. We notify our student body of your needs and requirements and forward to you the application materials of interested students approximately two weeks before your visit. After you have notified us of your selections, we arrange interviews at the campus of your choice.

  • Register for On-Campus Interviews using our Request On-Campus Interviews Online Registration Form.

Off-Campus Recruitment (Resume Only) Programs
The Career Development Office collects resumes of qualified students and forwards them to you, or you may choose to have students mail their materials directly to your office. Interviews may be arranged by you and take place on your premises at your convenience. If after receiving resumes you wish to schedule on-campus interviews, we will accommodate your request.

  • Register to receive resumes only using our Request to Receive Resumes Only Online Registration Form.

Employment Listings Service
This service is a year-round system that advises students and graduates of employment opportunities at your office as they arise. To utilize this program, simply contact our office at your discretion. Our alumni employment notices are published in our Graduate Newsletter weekly via e-mail and bi-monthly via hard copy. We advertise full, part-time and summer student employment opportunities at both of our campuses.

  • For more detailed information, please visito our Post a Job in our Job Bank page.
