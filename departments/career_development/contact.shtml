Contact the Career Development Office

The counseling process is particularly effective at Widener Law because all counselors have law degrees and have practiced in diverse settings. One-on-one counseling sessions, that take into account students' individual areas of interest and unique experiences, help students through each phase of their job search, from self-assesment through salary negotiation.

Through Widener's recruiting programs, employers visit each campus to interview students for summer and permanent positions. Students may participate in interviews on both campuses. The Office also maintains a catalog of employment openings throughout the year and participates in numerous job fairs.

Current students, alumni, prospective students and employers are encouraged to contact our Office with any comments or questions. Please contact the campus office most convenient for you:

  • Contact the Career Development Office in Delaware
  • Contact the Career Development Office in Harrisburg
