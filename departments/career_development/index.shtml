Welcome to Widener Law's Career Development Office!

"Our goal is to demystify the job search process by equipping our students with the information and skills necessary to find rewarding work."
» LeaNora Ruffin, Assistant Dean for Career Development
 
 

The Career Development Office (CDO) offers a full range of services to law students to facilitate the legal job search process. We understand that searching for legal employment can be an intimidating and time-consuming task. Therefore, our goal is to work with you to identify and formulate strategies to realize your career goals. We also provide services and sponsor workshops/programs designed to give you a solid foundation in basic job search skills as well as familiarize you with the wealth of career options available upon graduation from law school.
 
 

In Addition to Services for Widener Law Students, we offer:

  • Services For Legal Employers
  • Services For Alumni
  • Services For Prospective Students
  • Statistics: Our School and our Graduates

We maintain an open door policy and encourage students to stop by our office or make an appointment for quick questions or more concentrated assistance. Feel free to browse our web site to find out more information relevant to your job search!
