Tuition & Fees

2004-2005 Tuition and Fees

The following non-refundable admission fees are required at designated times during the application and administration process.

Application Fee: $60
(must accompany application)

Deposit: $400
(credited toward tuition)

Fees must be paid at the time they are due. Failure to do so will jeopardize an applicant's admission status and may result in the forfeiture of the School of Law's offer of admission.

The following tuition and fees must be paid when requested. Normally, first semester tuition and fees are due on July 15 and second semester tuition and fees are due on December 1.
