Student Dining: Delaware Campus

The food service is a contracted service through Aramark foodservice. They maintain two separate operations for the Delaware campus, one being the Barristers' Club and the other the Crown Court. Both operations are open to all individuals on campus.

The Barristers' Club

The Barristers' Club, houses the more traditional services with soups, sandwiches and cheese steaks made to order. It is located on campus and is open Monday through Friday with limited hours on the weekends. No specific food plan is required for residents.

Hours of Operation:
Monday through Friday: 11:30 am to 2:00 pm
Weekends: Limited hours

Crown Court

Crown Court is focused more on the grab and go variety of items. All service is on a al-carte basis.

Hours of Operation:
Monday through Friday: 8:00am to 11:00am and 3:00pm to 7:00 pm.
For more information call: (302) 477-3967

Catering

Catering If catering seems to be a need: a quick call to Jennifer at (302-477-2242) and she will help you with menus. All individuals requesting catering or special events that include foodservice are required to fill out a foodservice requisition with a department account number and the appropriate signatures.
