Delaware Mail Room

The Mail Room handles all the incoming and outgoing mail for the campus for all students, staff and faculty. It is located on the first floor of the law building for ease of access by the student population. Every full time student attending classes is issued a mailbox allowing direction mail distribution of important material to our students. The department also handles the mail that is sent inter-campus (to our Main campus in Chester, PA and our Harrisburg, PA campus).

Mrs. Arlene Dolan handles the day-to-day sorting; stuffing and posting of the mail, while Bob Fox and Barry Hinson are the people delivering the mail.

The window hours are Monday-Friday 8:30am-4: 30pm during the regular semester and as posted in the summer. The office phone number is 302-477- 2217.
