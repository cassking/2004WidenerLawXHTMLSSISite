Delaware Copy Center

Located on the first floor of the main Law building, the Copy Center produces the entire bulk copying, whether it is for student course material, the handbooks, large project copying or color brochures.

The primary users of the Copy Center are staff, faculty and student organizations.

People using our service are asked to fill out a copy request form and forward it to the Copy Center.
