Delaware Maintenance Department

The Maintenance Department, located on the east side of the campus, handles all the day-to-day work requests for the entire campus as well as overseeing the grounds. The entire campus is toured first thing in the morning (with the exception of the dormitories which are done after 9am) to get a jump on any problems before the class day starts. They also use a work order system, so jobs that can be scheduled. These forms are for all staff, faculty and students and should be filled out when maintenance problems occur. Mr. Frank Thompson, the maintenance supervisor; oversees a staff of 14 who perform the needed repairs as well as maintain the grounds.
