Harrisburg Copy Center

Located on the first floor of the student organization building the Copy Center produces bulk and large project copying. The primary users are staff, faculty and student organizations. People using our service are asked to fill out a copy request form and forward it to the Copy Center with the document(s) to be copied.

Student copying is done by the individual through use of the Coin-copiers located on the first floor of the Legal Information Center. Copy cards can be purchased through the vending machines in the Legal Information Center and the upstairs computer lab.

Student id/copy cards are available in the Media Services office located in the Legal Information Center on the first floor near the security desk. Please call Brian at (717) 541- 3963 to schedule an appointment for your photo.
