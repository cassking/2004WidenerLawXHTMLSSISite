Student Dining: Harrisburg Campus

The food service is a contracted service through Aramark foodservice, which is open to all individuals on campus.

The Side Bar Cafe'

The Side Bar Cafe' is located in the lower level of the student organization building. Menu selections include soups, made-to-order sandwiches, pizza, pre-made salads, fruit and desserts. The Cafe' provides a congenial meeting place for students, faculty and staff. The lounge area includes a comfortable seating area with a television, table tennis and foosball.

Hours of Operation:
Monday through Friday when classes are in session: 10:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. Hours may vary each semester. Vending and seating/lounge areas are open additional hours.
For more information call: (717) 541-3967

Catering

If catering is requested, call Brenda Rebuck at (717-541-3967) and she will help you with menus. All individuals requesting catering or special events that include foodservice are required to fill out a foodservice requisition form with a department account number and the appropriate signatures.
