Harrisburg Mail Room

The Mailroom handles all U.S. postal mail, UPS shipping and receiving and inter-campus mail. The office is located on the first floor of the student organization building.

The Harrisburg campus mailperson picks up and delivers mail twice a day to various locations on campus. U.S. postal mail and UPS packages are shipped and received Monday thru Friday. UPS packages shipped for next day service must be prepared and in the Mailroom by 2:00 p.m. for next-day delivery.
