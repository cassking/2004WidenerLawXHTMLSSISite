Harrisburg Maintenance Department

The Maintenance department in Harrisburg handles all the day-to-day work requests for the entire campus as well as overseeing the landscaping crew. Maintenance opens all the buildings in the morning and inspects all classrooms and all office areas. They use a work order system to schedule jobs accordingly. The forms are for all staff, faculty and students and should be filled out when maintenance problems occur. Bob Dolbin, the maintenance supervisor, oversees a staff of 2 who perform the needed repairs on campus. He also oversees a landscaping crew and the housekeeping staff.
