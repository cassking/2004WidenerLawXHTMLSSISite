Reporting Incidents: Delaware Campus

Any victim or witness of a crime who desires to report an incident on a voluntary and confidential basis may do so through the chief of Campus Safety, dean of students, and/or dean of the Law School. The individual's identity will be safeguarded when possible. The incident will be included in Widener's Crime Statistics. university professional and pastoral counselors will encourage victims and witnesses to report crimes on a voluntary and confidential basis if appropriate.

The Campus Safety Department closely cooperates with the Dean's Office and the Office of Student Academic Services to disseminate information about safety and security matters to the campus community through newsletters, annual brochures, and regularly scheduled staff meetings.

Representatives of the Campus Safety Department are among those who participate in regularly scheduled residence hall meetings conducted by the Residence Life staff. Personal safety and housing security and enforcement procedures are extensively covered during these meetings, which are designed to increase the residential students' understanding and awareness of the responsibilities of community living.
