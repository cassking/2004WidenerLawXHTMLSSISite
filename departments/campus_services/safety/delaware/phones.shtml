Courtesy Phones: Delaware Campus

Building, Location and Extension Number

Widener University has installed emergency phones on the Delaware campus. These phones are a compliment to the services offered by the University. They are located at:

  • Law Building:

  • Law Building:

    Lobby » Extension x3048
  • Shipley Dorm:
    Outside of Maintenance room » Extension x3048
  • Shipley Dorm:
    Room 316 (3rd floor TV room) » Extension x3082
  • Concord Dorm:
    Lobby » Extension x3032
  • Dining Center:
    ARAmark Food Line » Extension x3070

These phones dial Campus Safety automatically and report your location. To initiate a call, simply press the "Push for Help" button. When calls are initiated from these phones, a blue strobe light begins to flash. Immediately upon receipt of the call, a safety officer and other emergency response agencies will be dispatched to assist the caller.
