Widener University School of Law Campus Safety Department

The Campus Safety Department reports to the vice president of Administration and Finance and works closely with Campus Services. At Widener, safety is a primary consideration in maintenance, groundskeeping, and lighting. If a security patrol should discover a broken window or nonfunctional light, it informs Campus Services.

  • Campus Safety Department: Delaware Campus
  • Campus Safety Department: Harrisburg Campus

Safety Updates

The Campus Safety Department closely cooperates with the School of Law Dean's Office and the Office of Student Academic Services (Visit the Office of Student Affairs site) to disseminate information about safety and security matters to the campus community through notices, newsletters, and annual brochures. Members of the university community on both the Delaware and Harrisburg Campuses are welcome and encouraged to attend crime prevention programs offered at the university's Main Campus in Chester, Pa. In addition, the School of Law weekly newsletter, The Weekly News Digest , addresses the issue of campus safety and crime prevention, encouraging students to be aware and responsible for their own safety.
