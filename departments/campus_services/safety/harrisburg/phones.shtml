Courtesy Phones: Harrisburg Campus

Widener University has installed emergency phones on the Harrisburg campus. These phones are a compliment to the services offered by the University. They are located at:

Building, Location and Extension Number

  • Building 3800:
    At Safety Desk 1st Floor » Extension x1951
  • Building 3800:
    By classroom 206 (2nd Floor) » Extension x1951
  • Building 3737:
    On Nursing Side » Extension x1951
  • Administration Building:
    By Receptionist Desk » Extension x1951
  • Building 3805:
    By Secretaries Office 1st Floor » Extension x1951
  • Building 3805:
    Side Bar Café near Restrooms » Extension x1951

These phones dial Campus Safety automatically and report your location. To initiate a call, simply press the "Push for Help" button. When calls are initiated from these phones, a blue strobe light begins to flash. Immediately upon receipt of the call, a safety officer and other emergency response agencies will be dispatched to assist the caller.
