Reporting Incidents: Harrisburg Campus

All students, faculty, staff, and guests are expected to report any unusual or suspicious incidents to a security officer at the security desk located immediately inside the main entrance of the classroom/library building, or to the Office of the Vice Dean.

Following the investigation and evaluation of the incident, the investigating safety officer will file a written report with the university and external authorities, if appropriate. The Campus Safety Department works closely with the Susquehanna Township Police Department, who have law enforcement jurisdiction for the Harrisburg Campus. Those victimized have the opportunity to report incidents to the Police Department.

Any victim or witness of a crime who desires to report an incident on a voluntary and confidential basis may do so through the chief of Campus Safety, dean of students, and/or vice dean. The individual's identity will be safeguarded when possible. The incident will be included in Widener's Crime Statistics.
