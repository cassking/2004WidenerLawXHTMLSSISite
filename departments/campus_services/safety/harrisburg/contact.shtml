Campus Services



 

Contact Campus Safety: Harrisburg Campus

Centrally located on the ground floor of the library, the Campus Safety Desk is staffed by an officer from 4:00PM until midnight Monday through Friday. An officer is also present during times that the library is open on the weekends. The Department utilizes both highly visible vehicle and foot patrols to maintain the security of the campus. Officer escorts for individual protection are available if requested. Students may contact the Safety Office at 541-3948.
