Annual Alumni Awards Reception

Note:
The deadline for nominations for the 2004 Alumni Awards is Friday, July 30, 2004.

The Widener University School of Law Alumni Association recognizes its alumni, who have brought honor, recognition and distinction to the Law School through their remarkable accomplishments, at the Annual Alumni Awards Reception in December.

We invite you to play a vital role in the selection process. Please nominate an alumnus/a or faculty member for any of the following awards when you learn of someone who satisfies the criteria outlined below. Our awards include: Outstanding Alumnus/Alumna of the Year Award, Outstanding Service Award, Recent Alumni Award and Outstanding Faculty Award.

Outstanding Alumnus/Alumna of the Year Award

The purpose of the Outstanding Alumnus/Alumna of the Year Award is to commend an alumnus or alumna whom, through service to his or her community or profession, and/or by honors received, service rendered or other accomplishments, has brought honor, recognition and distinction to the Widener University School of Law.

Outstanding Service Award

Service awards are granted for outstanding alumni service to the Law School as a whole, to a particular concentration within the school, and/or to one’s profession where Widener Law School is involved in the service.

Recent Alumni Award

This award recognizes work by recent graduates who show energy and dedication as community leaders and provide invaluable service to the Widener University School of Law.

Outstanding Faculty Award

This award goes to the tenured faculty member who was believed to make the greatest contribution to the development of the Widener University School of Law Alumni.
