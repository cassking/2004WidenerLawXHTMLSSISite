2003 Alumni Golf Outing

The 10th Annual Widener University School of Law Golf Outing was held on April 28th at the du Pont Country Club in Wilmington, Delaware. Nearly ninety golfers enjoyed the day on the course. Thanks to the participants, the perfect weather and our many generous sponsors, this year's golf outing was an exceptional event.


Foursome: David Rohde '80L (second from the left) with the members of his foursome: Kevin English, Michael Poole and David O'Rourke.


"Golfers at the 8th Hole attempted a hole-in-one to win this BMW Z4 (sponsored by Otto's BMW.) Unfortunately, the sports car went back to the dealership at the end of the day..."
