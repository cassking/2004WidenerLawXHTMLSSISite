2003 Philadelphia Alumni Reception

President Jim Harris and his wife, Mary, enjoyed meeting the Law Alumni at the 2003 Annual Philadelphia Reception.


University Trustee and Law School Overseer, Gene McGurk ('78)  took a moment to discuss Law School Advancement with Deborah McCreery, Asst. Dean of Development/Alumni Relations.


Dean Douglas Ray welcomed Law School Overseer, Lee Solomon ('78) to this year's event.


Steve Barsamian, Class of '75, is President of Widener's Law School Alumni Association.


Michael Goldberg/Vice Dean, Leanora Ruffin/Asst. Dean of Career Development, Hercules Grigos ('02) and Gene McGurk/Trustee and Overseer ('78) enjoyed the evening at the Kimmel Center.

The 2003 Annual Philadelphia Reception was highly successful and well-attended. Held this year at the Kimmel Center in Center City on March 13th, nearly three hundred alumni, friends, staff and faculty attended  the event.



Philadelphia, PA, March 2003
