The Women's Law Caucus (WLC)

Harrisburg, Pennsylvania


The Women's Law Caucus (WLC) is open to all law students.

Its programs are designed to promote an atmosphere of equality amongst all students, provide support and help alleviate some of the pressures and anxieties of law school.


Primary goals of the WLC are:

  • To create a professional environment for women within the law school;
  • To further the academic and career goals of each woman;
  • To encourage the recognition and discussion of women's contributions to the legal field, both past and present;
  • To recognize women as integrated human beings, who balance professional, academic and personal responsibilities.
