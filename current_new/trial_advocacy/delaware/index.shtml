Moe Levine Trial Advocacy Honor Society

Wilmington, Delaware

The Moe Levine Trial Advocacy Honor Society is the Law School's honorary society for trial advocates. It is named for the late Morris (Moe) Levine, Esq. of New York City, a trial advocate who inspired much of the present growth of law school trial advocacy education. Members are chosen each year from among those students who excel in forensic trial advocacy. The Society sponsors the Hugh B. Pearce Memorial Trial Advocacy Competition as well as qualifying competitions for Gourley Moot Court, Texas Trial Lawyers, American Trial Lawyers Association and the National Association of Criminal Defense Counsel.
