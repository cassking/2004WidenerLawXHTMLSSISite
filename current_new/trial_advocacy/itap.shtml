Intensive Trial Advocacy Program.

ITAP is an eight-day intensive course conducted each year after the fall and spring semester final examinations. Highly experienced trial attorneys, judges and law professors make up the ITAP faculty. Distinguished local experts take part in ITAP's "expert witness day," which permits students to work with actual expert witnesses. The ITAP program culminates with a simulated jury trial.
