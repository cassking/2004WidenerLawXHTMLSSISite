Fitness Center Memberships

Wilmington, Delaware

Widener University School of Law's Student Bar Association and the Jewish Community Center of Wilmington, Delaware, have had a long-standing corporate membership.

Each year, the SBA renews this contract to provide all Widener Law students with a free membership to the JCC.

Each membership allows Widener students access to the Fitness Center, the basketball courts and the indoor pool.
