Moot Court Honor Society

The Moot Court Honor Society is a student-run organization at Widener University School of Law. Members of the Moot Court Board represent Widener School of Law at leading national and international moot court competitions. Further, the Moot Court Society's on both the Delaware and Harrisburg campuses host a number of Moot Court Competitions. Members of the Moot Court Honor Society are selected on the basis of grades, writing ability, and oral advocacy skills. Selected students participate in numerous national and regional moot court competitions.
