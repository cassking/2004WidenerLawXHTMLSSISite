Moot Court Society

Wilmington, Delaware

Students who demonstrate exceptional oral and written advocacy skills in the New Member Try Out Competition are invited to join the Society. Members are awarded academic credit for a variety of services to the organization, including participation in interscholastic competitions.

Appellate advocacy programs sponsored by the Society provide student participation in intrascholastic competitions such as the annual Honorable G. Fred DiBona Competition and the Delaware-Harrisburg Moot Court Competition. Members of the Society are selected to compete in Moot Court Interscholastic Competitions across the country.

  

Each spring, the society hosts the Ruby R. Vale Interschool Corporate Moot Court Competition.
