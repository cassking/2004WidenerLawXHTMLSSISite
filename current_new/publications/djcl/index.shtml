Delaware Journal of Corporate Law

Wilmington, Delaware

The Delaware Journal of Corporate Law was established in 1975 on the Wilmington Campus. The Journal publishes legal articles, notes and comments in the areas of corporate, business and banking law. It is published by a student editorial board and staff selected for their academic achievements, research skills and writing abilities. It is the only national law review that publishes unreported corporate opinions from the Delaware Court of Chancery. It has developed a reputation of excellence as a reference resource for the bench and bar.

The Journal's editorial board also sponsors the Francis G. Pileggi Distinguished Lecture in Law. Established in 1986, this annual lecture brings noted academics, jurists and practitioners to speak to the students and the bench and bar on various corporate topics
