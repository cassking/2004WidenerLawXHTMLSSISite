Widener Law Review

Wilmington, Delaware

The Widener Law Review (formerly The Widener Law Symposium Journal) was established in 1994 and is dedicated to exploring a different timely area of the law each year. The Widener Law Review is published by a student editorial board and staff selected for their academic achievements, research skills and writing ability and is based on a symposium held on the Wilmington Campus. Articles by symposium speakers form the core of the publication and student-authored pieces provide additional perspectives on the symposium topic. The first issue explored critical legal issues relating to the new health care economy
