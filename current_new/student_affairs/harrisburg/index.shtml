Office of Student Academic Affairs Harrisburg Campus

Harrisburg, Pennsylvania

Overview The Dean of Students Office on the Harrisburg Campus is dedicated to assisting law students with many facets of their law school student life. It is designed to provide a variety of services to help students develop personally and professionally. The Dean of Students provides counseling for students in the area of scheduling issues, academic issues, personal problems or issues that deal with balancing the stresses of law school and everyday life. Elizabeth Simcox currently serves as Dean of this office. The office serves all students on the Harrisburg Campus and addresses any issues that may arise regarding the accommodation of disabilities and academic support for students requiring assistance. Dean Simcox (Elizabeth.G.Simcox@law.widener.edu) also oversees all student organizations such as the Student Bar Association, Moot Court and Trial Advocacy Honor Societies, student interest organizations and she acts as advisor to the Widener Law Journal. Appointments with the Dean of Students may be made by calling Dorothy Koncar at 717-541-3952.
