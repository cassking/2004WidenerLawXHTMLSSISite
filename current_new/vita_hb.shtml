The Volunteer Income Tax Assistance Program (VITA)

Harrisburg, Pennsylvania

Mission

The mission of the Widener University School of Law VITA Program is to provide tax assistance to low and moderate income taxpayers. We assist taxpayers with Federal, State and Local Tax returns.

Services

In addition to tax preparation, we offer free eFile services for both Federal and Pennsylvania tax returns, including direct deposit of refunds. We offer our services by appointment, generally on Saturdays throughout the tax season, as well as other mutually agreeable times.

What Students Do

Students start by talking to the client and finding out what his/her particular situation is. This helps the student develop interview skills while providing the taxpayer with the appropriate services for his/her needs.

What we have accomplished

This last tax season we prepared over 130 Federal, State and local returns. Our comprehensive approach saved one taxpayer over $400 in Federal Income taxes when we caught a coding error on a 1099. We have even helped taxpayers get refunds due them from prior years by filing returns for previous years.
