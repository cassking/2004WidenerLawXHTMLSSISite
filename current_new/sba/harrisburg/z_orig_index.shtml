Widener - Harrisburg Student Bar Association

Harrisburg, Pennsylvania

The students at Widener - Harrisburg take part in campus activities, academic associations, community projects and social events. They have twice led their campus to be named "Outstanding School of the Year" by the ABA's Law Student Division. On this campus, you will find special interest societies, sports teams, service projects and co-curricular activities. Participation in the activities below demonstrates that Widener students are committed to both their law school and their community. With so many organizations there is a student activity for everyone.
