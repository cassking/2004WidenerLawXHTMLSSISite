Widener - Delaware Student Bar Association

Wilmington, Delaware

Students at Widener - Delaware are active in and out of the classroom. Students participate in a wide range of extra- and co-curricular as well as public service activities, experiencing law school beyond the four walls of the classroom. The list on the Delaware Student Organizations page demonstrates the wealth of special-interest, service, sport and academic activities available to the Widener - Delaware student.
