Alumni Donor Scholarships

Donor scholarships: Donor scholarships are endowments established through the generosity of alumni, friends, faculty, foundations, law firms and corporations. Since the founding of Widener University School of Law, our law students have benefited from the significant gifts of our many donors. The Law School is deeply grateful for their loyal support.
