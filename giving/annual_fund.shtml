Alumni Giving: Annual Law School Fund

The Law School Annual Fund is the yearly solicitation of our alumni. Your gifts directly support the scholarship funds, campus improvement, the student organizations and library improvements.

Your role in the Annual Giving effort: Make your gift today, just use our Online Pledge Form (Make A Pledge Online ) or E-mail the Alumni Director (law.alumni@law.widener.edu) with your pledge - the Law School will confirm your pledge in writing.

Volunteer to serve as a Firm Representative and solicit your associates and partners. Firms with 100% participation will be recognized in the Honor Roll of Donors and receive a plaque from the Law School.

Volunteer to serve as a Class Agent, sending a solicitation letter to fellow classmates and helping with the annual phonathon.
