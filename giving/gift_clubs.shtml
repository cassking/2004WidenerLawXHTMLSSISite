Alumni Giving: Donor Recognition

Widener University School of Law recognizes donors in many special ways. Annual giving is recognized as follows:

Partner's Club: $5,000 - $9,999

  • Dean's guest at the Annual Law School Holiday Party
  • Special Invitation to Law Benefactors Recognition Dinner at the Hotel du Pont
  • Honorary guest at the Annual Alumni Awards Reception
  • Guest at the Pileggi Lecture Breakfast
  • Invitation to Dean's Annual Donor Cocktail Reception at Law School
  • Guest pass to law school lectures and symposia
  • Law School Annual Report

Benefactor: $2,500 - $4,999

  • Special Invitation to Law Benefactors Recognition Dinner at the Hotel du Pont
  • Honorary guest at the Annual Alumni Awards Reception
  • Guest at the Pileggi Lecture Breakfast
  • Invitation to Dean's Annual Donor Cocktail Reception at Law School
  • Guest pass to law school lectures and symposia
  • Law School Annual Report

Founder's Club: $1,500 - $2,499

  • Honorary guest at the Annual Alumni Awards Reception
  • Guest at the Pileggi Lecture Breakfast
  • Invitation to Dean's Annual Donor Cocktail Reception at Law School
  • Guest pass to law school lectures and symposia
  • Law School Annual Report

Dean's Council: $1,000 - $1,499

  • Invitation to Dean's Annual Donor Cocktail Reception at Law School
  • Guest pass to law school lectures and symposia
  • Law School Annual Report

Law School Associates: $500 - $999

  • Guest pass to law school lectures and symposia
  • Law School Annual Report

Bench and Bar Club: $250 - $499

  • Law School Annual Report
  • Widener School of Law Decal

Century Club: $100- $249

  • Law School Annual Report
