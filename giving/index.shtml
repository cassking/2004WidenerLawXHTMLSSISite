Welcome to Office of Development!

Widener Law's Office of Development is committed to securing the resources and recruiting the volunteers to preserve the invaluable learning experience available at Widener University School of Law. Prospective employers, students, and benefactors look to the Law School's alumni involvement and contributions to assess the commitment of our alumni and, indirectly, the quality of the Widener University School of Law educational experience.

Because of your support and involvement, Widener University School of Law can offer:

  • Clinical training programs
  • Specialized certificates in a variety of exemplary programs
  • loan repayment assistance programs for needy law students
  • Merit-based donor scholarships
  • Library renovations
  • Mentoring for first-year law students

Your contribution makes a difference and you can always tailor your donation to your area of interest, including: clinical programs, the general Scholarship Fund, law reviews, the Library, moot court programs, the loan repayment program and others.
