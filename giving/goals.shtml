Our Goals: Fund Raising Priorities

Our Immediate Goal: Please call 302-477-2172 before June 30th, 2004 and help us reach our goal of $240,000. Thank you for your support. The Office of Development will be happy to take your credit card contribution over the phone.

Long-term Goals: One of the goals of the Widener University School of Law Office of Development is to promote the School of Law among our alumni by encouraging participation in giving to Widener and by creating a sense of community among our alumni through local and regional events, reunions, and activities. We are available to serve our alumni and donors by providing giving options and services at any time throughout the year. We are always looking for new events and activities and openly encourage our alumni to be involved in hosting local and regional events.

Another priority for us is to increase alumni giving. Your gift to the Annual Law School Fund can make a difference right now in the educational experience of Widener Law students by supporting student scholarships, student programs and competitions, library renovations and campus improvements.

Every Gift Counts!
