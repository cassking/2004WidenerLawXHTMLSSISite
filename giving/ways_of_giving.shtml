Ways of Giving

Each year, gifts to the Widener University School of Law Annual Fund play a critical role in important programs such as financial aid scholarships and campus renovations. The Library collection as well as umerous student organizations also depend on the contributions given to the Annual Fund.

Alumni and friends can contribute to the Widener University School of Law through a variety of methods. There are many ways of giving to Widener including:

Monetary Gifts

  • Make A Pledge Online
  • The Law School Annual Fund: Your gifts directly support the scholarship funds, campus improvement, the student organizations and library improvements.
  • Alumni Donor Scholarships: Donor scholarships are endowments established through the generosity of alumni, friends, faculty, foundations, law firms and corporations.
  • Leadership Gifts which entitle you to membership in a number of Gift Clubs

Non-Monetary Gifts

  • Volunteer to serve as a Firm Representative and solicit your associates and partners.
  • Volunteer to serve as a Class Agent, sending a solicitation letter to fellow classmates.
  • Mentor a First-year law student: The Program pairs first-year students with qualified, experienced alumni like you for a short, informal mentorship to introduce new law students to the legal profession.
