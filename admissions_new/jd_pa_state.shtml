Widener University School of Law/PA State System of Higher Education Partnership Agreement

The Harrisburg Campus of the School of Law and the Pennsylvania State System of Higher Education (SSHE) have a partnership agreement simplifying enrollment at the Harrisburg Campus for students at any of the fourteen SSHE Universities. The partnership includes a 3+3 Early Admit Joint Degree Program and an Express Admit Program, as well Dixon scholarships to eligible students.

  • Programs available include:
  • 3+3 Early Admit Joint Degree Program:
    Through the 3+3 Early Admit Joint Degree Program, SSHE students who qualify may enroll at the School of Law following their junior year. These students take both law and undergraduate courses in what would usually be their senior year of undergraduate study and complete their undergraduate degree at the end of their first year of legal studies. Students will receive both the Bachelor's and Juris Doctor degrees in six years instead of seven. Students must apply by April 1 to be eligible.
  • Express Admit Program:
    Through the Express Admit Program , students graduating from a SSHE university will be assured admission to the Harrisburg Campus, provided they graduate in the top 50 percent of their graduating class, score above the 50th percentile on the LSAT and meet all other qualifications for admission. Students must apply by April 1 to be eligible.
  • Dixon Scholarships:
    Dixon Scholarships , which provide 30 percent of the yearly tuition and fees, are available to eligible students accepted into the 3+3 or Express Admit programs. Students must apply by April 1 to be eligible.
