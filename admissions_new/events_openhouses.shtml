Admissions Open Houses

Both the Wilmington, Delaware and Harrisburg, Pennsylvania Campuses will be hosting open house events for prospective applicants on the following dates:

Saturday, October 23, 2004
Saturday, January 8, 2005

Open House event programs begin with registration at 9:45 a.m. The formal programs begin at 10:00 a.m. and continue until approximately 1:00 p.m.

Join us to learn more about:

  • The Admissions Process
  • Financial Aid
  • Career options
  • Clinical Programs
  • Housing options

The Admissions Office staff, faculty and students will be available throughout the program to answer your questions.
