Requirements for Completion of the Juris Doctor (J.D.) Degree Program

A student must complete a minimum of 87 semester hours of course work to receive the Juris Doctor degree. The Law School curriculum (Delaware campus curriculum, Harrisburg campus curriculum) is designed to ensure the development of a fundamental understanding of the law and of the legal process. Students may focus their studies on a particular area of law by selecting elective courses suited to their professional interests.
