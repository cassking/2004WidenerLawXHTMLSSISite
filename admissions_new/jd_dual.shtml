Dual Degree Programs

Widener offers three additional options for J.D. candidates who wish to further specialize and earn additional graduate degrees. Dual degree programs allow students to earn two degrees in a shorter period of time than it would to complete each degree separately.

  • Widener currently offers:
  • Juris Doctor - Master in Business Administration (J.D./M.B.A.) dual-degree
  • Juris Doctor - Doctor of Psychology (J.D./Psy.D.) dual-degree
  • Related Link:Institute for Graduate Clinical Psychology)
  • Juris Doctor - Masters of Science in Library Science (J.D./M.S.L.S.): Additionally, in cooperation with Clarion University of Pennsylvania, Widener offers a Juris Doctor-Masters of Science in Library Science (J.D./M.S.L.S.).
