J.D. Part-time/Extended Division

The Extended Division is an evening and day program at both campuses in which students complete all required coursework in four years. Our current Extended Division program required course curriculum mandates class attendance three nights per week. Upper class students typically maintain a schedule of three nights per week if they attend at least one summer session. Our Extended Division, day program schedule varies from year to year.

Tuition: $ 10,050 per semester
