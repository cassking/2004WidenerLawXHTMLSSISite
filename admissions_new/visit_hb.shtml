Schedule a Visit to our Harrisburg, Pennsylvania Campus

Please contact the Admissions office at (717)541-3903 or 1-888-WIDENER to schedule a visit to campus. Campus tours generally last twenty minutes and include classrooms, administrative offices, and the Law Library. During the academic year, visitors may also sit in on a first year class. Please call at least two days in advance for this option.

Please be aware that certain dates may be unavailable for visits during the school year.
