How to Apply If You Are a Previous Law School Attendee or a Transfer Student

Transfer students are admitted in any semester. The applicant must submit a certified law school transcript and letter of good standing from the dean of the prior law school.

A transfer student may receive credit for law school work completed with a grade of C, its equivalent or better. The School of Law does not give credit for more than 35 credit hours of prior law school work.
