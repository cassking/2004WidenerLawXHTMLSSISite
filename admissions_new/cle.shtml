Continuing Legal Eduction (CLE) Admissions

Designed to meet the on-going needs of practicing attorneys

Widener University School of Law seeks to provide high quality continuing legal education programs, designed to meet the on-going needs of practicing attorneys. Widener is committed to providing practical, relevant legal education. CLE provides attorneys with credit to fulfill their mandatory annual requirements as established in Pennsylvania and Delaware.
