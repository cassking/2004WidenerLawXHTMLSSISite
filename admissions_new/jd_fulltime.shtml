J.D. Full-time/Regular Division

The Regular Division is a full-time day program in Delaware and Harrisburg in which students complete all required coursework in three years. Students attend class five days a week the first year and are prohibited from working more than 20 hours a week throughout the three-year program.

Tuition: $13,400 per semester
