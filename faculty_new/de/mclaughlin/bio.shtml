Julia H. McLaughlin

B.A., Princeton University
J.D., University of Wisconsin, Madison Law School

Julia H. McLaughlin received her law degree from the University of Wisconsin Law School, where she served as a Note and Comment Editor for the University of Wisconsin Law Review.

Following her graduation from law school, Professor McLaughlin joined the Philadelphia law firm of Schnader Harrison Segal & Lewis as an associate. In 1988, Professor McLaughlin joined Schnader's Family Law Department and concentrated her practice in the area of antenuptial agreements, litigation and mediation. Professor McLaughlin left Schnader in 1998 to teach legal writing at Villanova Law School.

In 1999, Professor McLaughlin accepted a position to teach Legal Methods at Widener University School of Law. Professor McLaughlin is a member of the Philadelphia and Pennsylvania Bar Associations and serves as a course presenter for continuing legal education presentations.
