Iva Johnson Ferrell

B.A., University of Delaware
M.B.A., Rutgers Graduate School of Management
J.D., University of Pittsburgh School of Law

Iva Johnson Ferrell is a Legal Writing Professor at Widener's Delaware campus. Professor Ferrell received a B.A. in Economics from the University of Delaware in 1991; a Master of Business Administration in Marketing from Rutgers Graduate School of Management in 1994 and a J.D. from the University of Pittsburgh School of Law in 1997.

Following graduation from law school, Professor Ferrell was employed as associate attorney at Pepper Hamilton LLP in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, from 1997-2000; and as an associate attorney at Morgan, Lewis & Bockius LLP in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania from 2000-2001. Most recently, Professor Ferrell was employed as associate counsel for QVC, Inc. in West Chester, Pennsylvania.

Professor Ferrell is admitted to practice in New Jersey and is a member of the American Bar Association, the National Bar Association, the National Black MBA Association and the University of Pittsburgh School of Law Alumni Association.
