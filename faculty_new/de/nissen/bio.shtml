Alison M. Nissen

B.A., College of William and Mary
J.D., Rutgers-Camden Law School

Alison M. Nissen is a Legal Methods Professor at Widener's Delaware Campus. Professor Nissen is a 1991 honors graduate of Rutgers-Camden Law School, and earned her B.A., with high honors, from the College of William and Mary.

Following graduation from law school, Professor Nissen practiced with the firm of Capehart and Scatchard in Mt. Laurel, New Jersey.

Professor Nissen joined the faculty at Widener University School of Law in July 2000. Professor Nissen taught Advanced Brief Writing at Rutgers-Camden during the Summer 2000 semester.
