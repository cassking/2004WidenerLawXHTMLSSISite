Larry D. Barnett

B.A., University of California
M.S., Oregon State University
Ph.D., Florida State University
J.D., Temple University

Larry D. Barnett, Professor of Law at Widener University (Delaware campus), received a B.A. summa cum laude from the University of California at Los Angeles in 1961; an M.S. from Oregon State University in 1962; a Ph.D. from Florida State University in 1965; and a J.D. with Honors from the University of Florida College of Law in 1975. Professor Barnett is admitted to practice in Florida.

Professor Barnett joined the faculty of the Widener University Law School in 1978 and was a Fulbright Scholar at the University of Leiden in The Netherlands in 1979-80. In addition to teaching professional responsibility, Professor Barnett offers a course on legal aspects of mutual funds and a course on the securities regulation.

Professor Barnett was also the founder and first editor-in-chief of the demography journal Population Research and Policy Review.
