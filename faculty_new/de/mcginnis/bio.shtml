Doretta Massardo McGinnis

A.B., Harvard University
J.D., University of Pennsylvania Law School

Doretta Massardo McGinnis is Legal Writing Professor at Widener's Delaware campus. Professor McGinnis received an A.B. from Harvard University in 1985, and a J.D. from University of Pennsylvania Law School in 1991.

Following graduation from law school, Professor McGinnis served as Associate Attorney, Pepper, Hamilton & Scheetz, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, from 1991-95, and joined the faculty at Widener in 1995 as Legal Methods Professor.

Professor McGinnis is admitted to practice in Pennsylvania. She teaches and writes in the areas of Legal Methods and Current Issues in Labor Law.
