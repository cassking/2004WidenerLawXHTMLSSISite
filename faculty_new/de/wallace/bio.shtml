Teresa A. Wallace

A.B., Bryn Mawr College
J.D., University of Pennsylvania Law School

Teresa A. Wallace is Associate Professor of Law and Director of Academic Services in the Office of Student Affairs at Widener's Delaware Campus. Professor Wallace received an A.B. degree from Bryn Mawr College in 1979, and her J.D. degree from the University of Pennsylvania Law School in 1984.

Following graduation from law school, Professor Wallace worked as an associate at Toll, Ebby and Langer, a Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, law firm, where she specialized in complex civil and criminal litigation.

Professor Wallace joined the faculty at Widener in 1993 as a Legal Methods Professor and, in 1995, was named the first full-time Director of Academic Support for the Delaware campus.

In 1997-1998, Professor Wallace studied Evidence and English Criminal Procedure at University College London and the London School of Economics. She returned to the faculty in 1999 and teaches Evidence, in addition to directing the Academic Services Program.
