Peter M. van Zante

B.A., Colorado College
J.D., University of Chicago School of Law

Peter M. van Zante is Associate Professor of Law at Widener's Harrisburg campus. Professor van Zante received a B.A. from Colorado College in 1968 and a J.D. from University of Chicago School of Law in 1971, where he served as Comment Editor, University of Chicago Law Review.

Following graduation from law school, Professor van Zante practiced law with Hutchinson, Black & Cook in Boulder, Colorado, from 1971-93. He also worked as an Intern with Koch, von Braunschweig & von Mettenheim in Frankfurt/Main Germany from 1982-83, and as a Visiting Professor at University of Iowa Law School in 1991.

Professor van Zante joined the faculty at Widener and has served as Associate Professor of Law since 1993. Professor van Zante is admitted to practice in Colorado. He teaches and writes in the areas of Federal Income Tax, Federal Estate and Gift Tax, ERISA, Pensions & Deferred Compensation, Business Practice, and Partnership and Sub S Tax.

Professor van Zante is on Leave of Absence from the Law School for academic year 2003-2004 and is teaching at Chapman University School of Law.
