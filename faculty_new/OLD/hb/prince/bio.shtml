Samantha J. Prince

B.S., Muhlenberg College
J.D., Widener University School of Law
LL.M., Georgetown University Law Center

Samantha J. Prince is a Visiting Associate Professor of Law at Widener's Harrisburg campus. Professor Prince received a B.S. from Muhlenberg College, Allentown, Pennsylvania, in 1989; a J.D., cum laude, from Widener University School of Law, Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, in 1992; and an LL.M. in Taxation from Georgetown University Law Center, Washington, D.C. in 1993.

Professor Prince was a Senior Associate at Hedger & Hedger in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, and has worked as a Law Clerk at Lipkin, Marshall, Bohorad & Thornburg in Pottsville, Pennsylvania, and also as a Legal Intern in the Harrisburg Civil Law Clinic in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. Professor Prince is admitted to practice in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.

Professor Prince joins Widener University School of Law in Spring 2004 as a Visiting Associate Professor of Law and will teach Securities Regulation and Taxation of Business Enterprises.
