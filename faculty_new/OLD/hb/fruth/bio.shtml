Ann Elizabeth Fruth

B.A., York College of Pennsylvania
M.A., University of Chicago
J.D., Widener University School of Law

Ann Elizabeth Fruth is a Legal Writing Professor at Widener's Harrisburg campus. Professor Fruth was Dean of Students for the Harrisburg Campus of the Widener University School of Law from 1995 until 2002.

Professor Fruth received a B.A. from York College of Pennsylvania, an M.A. from the University of Chicago, and a J.D. from Widener University School of Law. She is admitted to practice in Pennsylvania.

Following graduation from law school, Professor Fruth was legal counsel for the Pennsylvania Department of Banking. She joined the administration at Widener University School of Law in 1995.

Professor Fruth is a member of a number of professional and civic organizations, including the American Bar Association, the Pennsylvania Bar Association and the Dauphin Bar Association. She has represented this law school at the Law School Admissions Council, the Northeast Association of Prelaw Advisors, and the Western Association of Prelaw Advisors.

Professor Fruth serves as a member of the Advisory Board of Highmark, Inc., the consolidated company of Pennsylvania Blue Shield and Blue Cross of Western Pennsylvania. She is a member of the Board of Directors of the Hawk Mountain Sanctuary, an organization devoted to the conservation of raptors and their habitat worldwide.

Professor Fruth has done pro bono work for the Pennsylvania Asylum Resource Center.
