Making the Widener University School of Law Accessible to All

Many Internet users with disabilities find websites difficult or even impossible to use simply because of the way they are designed.

We are working hard to make our site compatible with various types of adaptive technology used by people with disabilities and are currently redesigning our page templates with clearer text and improved terminology ('click here' links should soon be consigned to history).

If you come across a part of our site that is difficult to use or could be improved in terms of accessibility we apologise and would be very grateful if you could e-mail us and let us know. Your feedback is an invaluable part of the process of improving our site.
