Using This Website: Technical Requirements

The following information may help you resolve some common issues when viewing the Widener University School of Law website and ensure full functionality.

Your browser

This website is best viewed with Microsoft Internet Explorer (IE) 5.5 and above or Netscape 6.2 and above, on both Microsoft Windows and Apple Macintosh computers. If you would like to upgrade to the latest version of your browser, you can get a version of Microsoft Internet Explorer at http://www.microsoft.com or a version of Netscape at http://www.netscape.com.

Recommended Browsers

Our site is designed to work on Internet Explorer 5.5 or higher and Netscape Navigator 6 or higher. We recommend Internet Explorer 5.5 or higher. We do not currently support WebTV. If you're using another browser, it must be able to accept frames, and JavaScript must be enabled. Our version recommendations for other browsers: Windows: Mozilla 1.4 or better Macintosh (System X): Netscape Navigator 6 or better Mozilla 1.4 or better Safari 1.2 or better Macintosh Classic (System 9): Netscape Navigator 6 or better Mozilla 1.4 or better Linux: Netscape Navigator 6 or better Mozilla 1.4 or better

Screen resolution

For best results when viewing this website, we suggest you set your screen size to 800 x 600 pixels or higher. For instructions on changing your desktop settings, refer to Help from the Start menu of your computer.
