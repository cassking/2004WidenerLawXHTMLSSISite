Office of the Dean, Harrisburg Campus

Welcome to the Widener University School of Law website. Designed and written by staff, students and faculty, is intended to provide you with a wide range of information about the School of Law. We will be happy to provide further information by telephone, e-mail or letter.

Widener is a unique law school in that we have two campuses. Our campus in Wilmington, Delaware, is home to approximately 1100 law students as well as our graduate programs in health law and corporate law and the Legal Education Institute which provides paralegal and court administration training. Our campus in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, is home to approximately 400 law students. Both provide an excellent legal education and our two campus arrangement adds strength and support to each. Both campuses are located in legal centers that offer many opportunities for full-time and part-time legal employment as well as internships with government agencies and courts.

We have an outstanding faculty made up of people who are excellent classroom teachers devoted to their students' success. In addition, they are experts in their fields, publishing articles in leading law journals as well as casebooks and treatises used by lawyers, law faculty and students. Our students, too, are very successful. Whether working together in our clinics and trial programs, representing us in national moot court competitions or writing for our excellent law journals, they are building a strong foundation for the future.

I invite you to review the information in these pages to learn more about our school. When you have done so, I hope that you will come visit us. We look forward to hearing from you.
