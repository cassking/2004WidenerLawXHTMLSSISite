Driving Directions


Detailed map of the area available through MapQuest.

From Philadelphia and Points North: I-95 south to Exit 8/Route 202 N. - Concord Pike.* From Baltimore and Points South: I-95 north to Exit 8/Route 202 N. - Concord Pike.* From Pennsylvania Turnpike (Route 76): Take Exit 23/Downingtown off the turnpike and follow Route 100 South to Route 202 (approximately 15 minutes). Travel south on Route 202 into Delaware. Two miles past the state line, on the left side of Route 202, is the Delaware Campus. From New Jersey: New Jersey Turnpike, 295 South or 40 West to Delaware Memorial Bridge. Take I-95 North to Exit 8/Route 202-Concord Pike* *The Delaware Campus is five miles north of the exit on the right side of Route 202.
