Dean's Welcome

Dean Douglas E. Ray

Welcome to the Widener University School of Law Delaware Campus website. Located in America's "corporate capital," our almost 1100 full-time and part-time students enjoy easy access to a wide range of employment and cultural opportunities in Wilmington, Philadelphia, Baltimore, Washington, D.C., and New York City. With an extensive law library, wireless internet access , modern classrooms with laptop plugins and display technology, a forty-acre campus which includes residential and dining facilities, and highly qualified faculty members who care about teaching, we offer an ideal learning environment.

Special features include a nationally-ranked Health Law Institute, an Institute for Delaware Corporate Law and a Trial Advocacy Institute which offers our Intensive Trial Advocacy Program. All offer innovative programs and provide students the opportunity to earn program certificates. Students can participate in two law journals, moot court and trial advocacy programs, law clinics representing low income people in Delaware and Pennsylvania, a Veterans Clinic and an Environmental Law Clinic. In addition, we offer LL.M. programs in health law and corporate law and a Legal Education Institute which provides paralegal and legal nurse consultant training.

I invite you to review the information on these pages to learn more about us. When you have done so, I hope that you will come visit us. We look forward to hearing from you.
