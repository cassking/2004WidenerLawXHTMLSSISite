About This Website

The Widener University School of Law website (About this website) aims to be accessible (Read more about Accessibility on our site) to all users including people with disabilities for whom the internet can be a very valuable communication and information tool. The degree to which our web pages are accessible to users with disabilities is defined in terms of priority levels and all new pages and sites within the Widner Law website will be assembled to be universally accessible at the priority 1 level. Our goal is to achieve accessibility at priority levels 2 and 3.
