Two Campuses, One Great School

Welcome!

Widener is a unique law school in that we have two campuses. Both provide an excellent legal education and our two campus arrangement adds strength and support to each.

Wilmington, Delaware

Our campus in Wilmington, Delaware, is home to approximately 1100 law students as well as our Graduate Programs in Health Law and Corporate Law; our Summer Abroad Institutes in Kenya, Italy, Switzerland and Australia; our Clinics and Externship programs which offer our students hands-on legal experience in real cases involving indigent clients, environmental causes and government service; and our Health Law Institute, Trial Advocacy Institute and The Institute of Delaware Corporate Law that allow our students to earn a certificate in specialized legal training and education.

Harrisburg, Pennsylvania

Our campus in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, is home to approximately 400 law students; Clinics and Externship programs that offer our students hands-on legal experience in real cases involving indigent clients, environmental causes and government service; and our Law & Government Institute that provides specialized legal training for students interested in representing government branches and agencies as well as citizens in litigation with government branches and agencies.
